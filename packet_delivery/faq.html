Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What's the goal?

A: Read the full instructions listed on the instructions page. You must complete the "Where Credit is Due" upgrade to complete the game.

 

Q: The audio is too loud/soft. How do I change this?

A: There are audio controls on the home screen as well as on the in-game menu via the Escape button.

 

Q: How do I quit?

A: If you need to quit mid-game, press Escape on your keyboard, then press "Quit To Title," and then press "Confirm." There is also an option to quit on the title screen.

 

Q: This upgrade is in my way. How do I hide it?

A: You can toggle each upgrade to show/hide it. Visit the upgrades menu and click on the appropriate upgrade. It'll show you at the top of the screen which button to press to toggle it.

 

Q: I forgot how to do something. What do I do?

A: Press Escape on your keyboard and select "Instructions." For upgrades, visit the Upgrades menu at your in-game office.

 

Q: I have a friend that might be interested. Can I share this link?

A: No. Please reach out directly to me first and I will determine if this friend is a potential study participant first. If they are not, we may discuss steps moving forward at that time.
