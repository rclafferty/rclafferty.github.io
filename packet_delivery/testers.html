Packet Delivery - Master's Thesis Game

Thank you for your willingness to participate in my study! I'm Casey Lafferty, a current masters student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln researching gamification. This study is designed to analyze the effect of using an educational video game to teach a Computer Science topic. Detailed instructions are provided below. If you have any questions about the instructions, the materials, or general questions, please contact me via email at rclafferty@huskers.unl.edu and I'll be glad to help.

 

Should you wish to discuss this game with me or other testers, you're welcome to email me at rclafferty@huskers.unl.edu or join my discord server at https://discord.gg/7Agub5F. You may freely discuss whatever you wish about the game on that server. If you have friends who would like to be testers, please contact me and let me know directly so that I may verify they're not potential study participants.

 

System Requirements:

  • This game is supported on Windows 7/8/10 or Mac OSX operating systems.
  • It is recommended you use a computer with at least an Intel i5 or AMD FX 6300 processor and at least 4 GB of RAM.
  • A graphics card will help with graphical performance but is not required to run this game.

 

Testing Instructions:

  • Think very critiquely when testing this game. You'll be asked to provide feedback on how to improve certain elements of the game.
  • Starting now through the end of the study, do not share any game content with other people outside of my discord server. It is likely that you know potential study participants. If you have friends who would like to be testers, please contact me and let me know directly so that I may verify they're not potential study participants.
  • Fill out the feedback form (via the link below) when you've completed the game.
  • If any questions are unclear, feel free to reach out directly to me at rclafferty@huskers.unl.edu for clarification.

 

Game Instructions:

  • This game is to teach the DNS Lookup process. If you're unfamiliar with DNS lookup, please do not feel intimidated.
  • You must complete the "Where Credit is Due" upgrade to complete the game.
  • Controls will be explained in-game, requiring only a mouse and keyboard.

 

By continuing, you agree that you've read and understand the above instructions. If you feel you do not agree or have questions, please contact me first before playing the game.
